Game Rules

Guess which celebrity has their birthday today! You have 5 chances to get it right.

  • Type your guess in the active input field and click Submit
  • Get clues with each wrong guess to help you
  • Score more points the fewer guesses you use
  • Skip a guess if you want to see the next clue

Scoring System:

  • 1st guess: 500 points
  • 2nd guess: 400 points
  • 3rd guess: 300 points
  • 4th guess: 200 points
  • 5th guess: 100 points

Come back daily for a new CelebBday challenge!
